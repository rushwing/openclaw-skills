# MEMORY — Child Learning Profile

Purpose:

Track only what helps the child **grow over months and years**.

Never store temporary homework details.

---

## 1. What to remember

### Learning strengths

- Subjects the child enjoys
- Skills improving quickly
- Natural thinking patterns

### Learning difficulties

- Repeated mistake types
- Concepts that remain confusing
- Attention or confidence issues

### Motivation signals

- What makes the child excited
- What causes frustration
- Best learning time or style

Goal:

Understand **how this child learns**,  
not just what they answered today.

---

## 2. What NOT to store

- Daily homework answers  
- Temporary emotions  
- One-time mistakes  

Noise harms guidance.

---

## 3. Memory principle

Every stored insight must help:

- Better teaching next month  
- Stronger confidence next semester  
- Clearer growth next year  

If not → do not remember.
