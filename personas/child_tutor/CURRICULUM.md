# CURRICULUM ROADMAP (Grade 4 → Grade 6)

Goal:

Build three long-term abilities:

1. Logical thinking (Math)
2. Language expression (Chinese)
3. Practical communication (English)

Not exam tricks.  
**Real cognitive growth.**

---

## Stage 1 — Grade 4 Foundation

### Math

Focus:

- Multi-step word problems
- Fractions basics
- Area & perimeter intuition

Core ability:

**Turn words into equations.**

---

### Chinese

Focus:

- Paragraph understanding
- Retelling stories
- Writing complete sentences

Core ability:

**Express clear meaning.**

---

### English

Focus:

- Daily conversation
- Simple sentence patterns
- Listening confidence

Core ability:

**Dare to speak.**

---

## Stage 2 — Grade 5 Expansion

### Math

- Fractions & decimals mastery
- Logical reasoning problems
- Early abstraction

Goal:

**Independent problem solving.**

---

### Chinese

- Reading comprehension depth
- Short structured writing
- Emotion & viewpoint expression

Goal:

**Write something meaningful.**

---

### English

- Dialogue fluency
- Basic grammar awareness
- Short paragraph speaking

Goal:

**Communicate ideas, not single words.**

---

## Stage 3 — Grade 6 Transition

### Math

- Ratio & proportion
- Beginning algebra thinking
- Complex multi-step reasoning

Goal:

**Prepare for middle school logic jump.**

---

### Chinese

- Analytical reading
- Structured composition
- Clear argument expression

Goal:

**Think → then write.**

---

### English

- Smooth conversation
- Paragraph listening & speaking
- Confidence in real use

Goal:

**Use English as a tool, not a subject.**

---

## Final principle

If the child gains:

- Confidence  
- Curiosity  
- Thinking habits  

Then grades will follow naturally.
